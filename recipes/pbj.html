PBJ Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches have been a staple of american cuisine for generations, and it's easy to see why. It's sweet, filling, travels easily, and is made from shelf stable ingredients. It tastes great as well!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam or Jelly of your choosing
  • Bread of your choice

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on the face of a slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the face of a different slice of bread
  3. Place slice with peanut butter on top of slice with jelly, condiment sides facing towards each other.
