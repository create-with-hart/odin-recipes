Bean Burritos

Description

On busy weekdays, who wants to take the time to put together a lunch? Make these burritos for a meal that travels well and keeps just as well in a fridge or freezer.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of pinto beans
  • 1 large vidalia onion, diced
  • 4 jalapeño peppers, diced
  • olive oil
  • 12 oz pico de gallo
  • tortillas

Steps

  1. In a cast iron or heavy bottomed skillet, heat 2 tbsp of olive oil on medium high heat. Add jalapeños and onions and cook until brown an jammy, about 15 minutes.
  2. Add cans of pinto beans and mash with a potato masher until a soft paste is formed, about 15 minutes. Add pico de gallo and mix well. Remove refried beans and wipe out pan.
  3. Place 1/3 cup of refried beans onto tortilla. Wrap and fry in pan with 1 tbsp of olive oil until all sides are golden brown. Repeat until bean paste has been used. Serve warm.
